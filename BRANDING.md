# Donna Spa — Brand Guide

## Brand essence
**Donna Spa Bakung Sari** is an authentic Balinese wellness sanctuary in the heart of Kuta,
part of the **Carla Spa Group**. It offers heartfelt Balinese hospitality, skilled therapists,
and a clean, calming escape from the busy streets of Kuta — at honest, fair prices.

- **Positioning:** Premium-yet-approachable neighbourhood spa for travellers in Kuta.
- **Personality:** Serene · Warm · Tropical · Natural · Caring.
- **Promise:** *"Leave refreshed."*

## Voice & tagline
- Primary tagline: **"A Sanctuary of Balinese Calm"**
- Indonesian touch: *"Rasa tenang khas Bali."*
- Tone: warm, unhurried, sensory. Bilingual EN-first (Kuta is international), with Balinese terms
  (*lulur*, *creambath*, *kamboja*).

## Typography
- **Display / headings:** *Fraunces* (boutique editorial serif).
- **Body / UI:** *Plus Jakarta Sans*.

## Motif
- Frangipani (*kamboja*) flower & tropical leaf line-art.
- Soft organic blob shapes, subtle paper grain, generous whitespace.
- Placeholder imagery rendered as bespoke SVG/gradient graphics (no stock photos yet).

## Logo
Wordmark **Donna** in Fraunces italic + `SPA` letterspaced, paired with a minimal frangipani mark.
